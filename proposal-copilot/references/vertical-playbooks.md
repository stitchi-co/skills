# Vertical Playbooks

Industry-specific guidance for tailoring proposals. Select the closest match; combine if the client spans verticals.

## Construction / Trades / Engineering

**Key personas:** Field crews (hi-vis/safety), office staff (corporate wear), recruiting (career fair swag), client relations (milestone gifts)
**Lead with:** Safety compliance (ANSI-rated gear), jobsite durability, multi-location delivery to job sites
**Language cues:** "job sites", "safety stand-downs", "topping-out ceremonies", "PPE", "crews"
**Merch emphasis:** Hi-vis everything, hard hat stickers, rugged drinkware, weather gear, safety milestone recognition
**Case study fit:** Little Ghost (high-volume, quality-cost balance), RevolutionParts (fulfillment efficiency)
**Objection to pre-empt:** "Can your products meet ANSI/safety standards?" → Yes, we source ANSI-rated apparel and can provide compliance documentation.

## Technology / SaaS

**Key personas:** Engineers (understated, quality-focused), sales teams (client-facing polish), recruiting (career fair + intern programs), remote employees (shipped-to-home)
**Lead with:** Platform technology (SSO, API, automation), remote-friendly fulfillment, brand consistency at scale
**Language cues:** "onboarding kits", "remote teams", "swag-as-a-service", "developer experience", "employer brand"
**Merch emphasis:** Premium brands (Patagonia, Nike), minimalist design, tech accessories, shipped-to-home kits
**Case study fit:** Porter (sales enablement), Morning Brew (campaign-driven, cost savings at scale)
**Objection to pre-empt:** "We need SSO and API access" → Included in Premium tier, SAML/OIDC/Google supported.

## Healthcare / Life Sciences

**Key personas:** Clinical staff (scrubs-adjacent, comfort wear), admin (corporate), patients/community (awareness events), pharma reps (client gifting)
**Lead with:** Compliance awareness, infection control considerations, comfort/durability for long shifts
**Language cues:** "shifts", "clinical staff", "patient experience", "awareness campaigns", "compliance"
**Merch emphasis:** Comfort-focused apparel, antimicrobial options where available, name-badge accessories, wellness items
**Objection to pre-empt:** "Are your products safe for clinical environments?" → We source from medical-adjacent suppliers and can restrict catalog to appropriate items.

## Financial Services / Professional Services

**Key personas:** Client-facing professionals (polished, conservative), support staff, recruiting (campus), client gifting (relationship management)
**Lead with:** Brand consistency, premium quality, compliance (no flashy/inappropriate items), executive gifting
**Language cues:** "client meetings", "corporate identity", "compliance", "professional image", "relationship building"
**Merch emphasis:** Premium polos, outerwear, leather goods, engraved accessories, high-end gift boxes
**Objection to pre-empt:** "We need approval workflows for compliance" → Built-in, configurable per department/spend level.

## Manufacturing / Industrial

**Key personas:** Floor workers (durable, safety), office staff, shipping/logistics, maintenance crews
**Lead with:** Durability, safety compliance, multi-shift coverage, seasonal gear rotation
**Language cues:** "floor", "shifts", "PPE", "plant", "distribution center"
**Merch emphasis:** Work-rated apparel, layering systems, safety accessories, insulated gear, durable bags
**Case study fit:** RevolutionParts (fulfillment), Little Ghost (production quality at scale)

## Hospitality / Food & Beverage

**Key personas:** Front-of-house (guest-facing, brand expression), back-of-house (functional), management, event staff
**Lead with:** Brand expression through uniforms, guest experience touchpoints, seasonal/event flexibility
**Language cues:** "guest experience", "front-of-house", "uniforms", "seasonal menus", "activations"
**Merch emphasis:** Branded aprons, server wear, guest gifts, event-specific items, packaging
**Case study fit:** San Morello (fast implementation), Noho Hospitality (brand consistency across venues), Little Ghost (packaging)

## Education / Nonprofit

**Key personas:** Students/members, staff/faculty, donors, volunteers, event attendees
**Lead with:** Budget efficiency, community building, mission-aligned sourcing (eco-friendly), event support
**Language cues:** "community", "mission", "donors", "volunteers", "school spirit", "awareness"
**Merch emphasis:** Eco-friendly options, bulk-friendly items, spirit wear, donor recognition gifts, event giveaways
**Objection to pre-empt:** "We're budget-constrained" → Free hosting at $100K+ spend, scalable packages, and our curation ensures you're not overspending on products that collect dust.

## How to Use

1. Identify client's primary vertical
2. Pull relevant personas, language, and emphasis into proposal
3. Select matching case studies
4. Pre-empt the listed objection in your exec summary or relevant section
5. Adapt — most clients span 2 verticals (e.g., "tech company with field operations")
