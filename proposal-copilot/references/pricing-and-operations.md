### Standard vs Premium

Standard includes: shopping cart, custom domain/SSL, inventory, account balances, coupons, gift cards, permissions, approvals, custom data collection, zip code tax, order push, shipping integrations, accounting integrations (ShipStation, QuickBooks, ProfitMaker, SmartBooks), APIs (up to 15K hits).

Premium adds: group passcode, IP restrictions, Avalara tax, SSO (Google/JSON/SAML), ACH, Points, tiered pricing, APIs (up to 30K hits).
