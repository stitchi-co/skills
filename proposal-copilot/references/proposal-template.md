# Proposal Template — Section Structure

Current proposal format extracted from the Company Store PandaDoc template.
Sections marked **[CUSTOM]** require per-client tailoring. All others are **[BOILERPLATE]** (reusable across proposals with minor tweaks).

## Section Order

1. **Cover Page** [BOILERPLATE] — Document type label + `[Client.Company]` branding
2. **Executive Summary** [CUSTOM] — Personalized pitch: client pain points, Stitchi solution highlights, key program benefits (5 bullet points typical)
3. **Stakeholder Personas & Merch Matrix** [CUSTOM] — 4–6 personas with event contexts and product recommendations per persona
4. **Who We Are** [BOILERPLATE] — Founding story, team photos, purpose/values/future
5. **Why Stitchi / Differentiators** [BOILERPLATE] — Passion for Quality, Local & Accountable, Customer-First Programs, Responsible & Sustainable
6. **Our Reach** [BOILERPLATE] — Stats (175K+ items fulfilled, 500 vetted suppliers, 22 manufacturing partners), office locations
7. **Company Store Highlights** [BOILERPLATE] — Platform capability overview (serve any customer, custom experiences, integrations)
8. **Company Store Features** [BOILERPLATE] — Detailed feature grid (access/roles, catalog, pricing/stipends, approvals, checkout, reporting, integrations, content/brand, returns, launch/governance)
9. **Security & Compliance** [BOILERPLATE] — SOC-2, Google Cloud Platform, Stripe PCI, OMG Trust Center
10. **Store Hosting Pricing** [BOILERPLATE] — Standard vs Premium tier comparison table + add-ons (Punchout, Virtual Samples, Budgets, Zapier, Demo Store, Micro Store, SAML SSO)
11. **Store Build Packages** [BOILERPLATE] — Package 1 ($1,598 / 6h design / 5 products), Package 2 ($2,398 / 6h design / 25 products), add-on design hours ($129/hr)
12. **Implementation Timeline** [BOILERPLATE] — 8-week phased rollout (Kickoff → Setup → Review → Launch Prep → Go-Live → Post-Launch)
13. **Warehousing & Fulfillment** [BOILERPLATE] — Storage pricing by item size, fulfillment rates, inbound shipment rates
14. **Service Commitment** [BOILERPLATE] — Dedicated AM, support hours (Mon–Fri 8–5 ET), SLAs, employee help, catalog management, on-site experiences, change notifications
15. **Partners / Social Proof** [BOILERPLATE] — Logo wall
16. **Case Studies** [SEMI-CUSTOM] — 4 mini case studies selected for relevance to client industry/use case
17. **Onboarding Experience** [BOILERPLATE] — 6-step visual flow (Kickoff → Store Setup → Product Selection → Training → Launch → Continuous Improvement) + primary contact form
18. **Signature Page** [BOILERPLATE] — Agreement terms, account info, billing info, dual signature blocks

## Key Patterns

- **Executive Summary** is the highest-value custom section — leads with client's specific priorities, then maps Stitchi's solution to those priorities using 5 bullet points
- **Stakeholder Personas** section is the main consultative differentiator — shows Stitchi understands the client's organization, not just their merch needs
- **Pricing sections** are transparent and structured — builds trust by showing exact rates upfront
- **Tone**: consultative, confident, partnership-oriented. "We're your merch team, not a swag vendor."
- **Footer**: every page carries Stitchi address + "Proposal for [Client.CompanyShort]"
